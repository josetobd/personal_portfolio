Books

Books

Of the various instruments invented by man, the most amazing is the book; all others are extensions of his body … Only the book is an extension of the imagination and memory. Jorge Luis Borges.

In this section, I will collect some of my favourite books

Buy Beating the Street by Peter Lynch

Beating the Street

Beating the Street by Peter Lynch is a practical guide that teaches investors how to identify companies with exceptional growth potential through his unique approach and accessible strategies.

Buy The Intelligent Investor by Benjamin Graham

The Intelligent Investor

The Intelligent Investor by Benjamin Graham is a timeless guide that emphasizes value investing principles, teaching readers how to make smart, long-term investment decisions while managing risk.

Buy The Lean Startup by Eric Ries

The Lean Startup

The Lean Startup by Eric Ries offers a groundbreaking approach to building businesses and products, emphasizing iterative design, validated learning, and agility to succeed in an uncertain market.

Buy Barbarians at the Gate by Bryan Burrough and John Helyar

Barbarians at the Gate

Barbarians at the Gate by Bryan Burrough and John Helyar chronicles the gripping and dramatic story of the leveraged buyout of RJR Nabisco, capturing the excess and ambition of 1980s Wall Street.

Buy Principles: Life and Work by Ray Dalio

Principles: Life and Work

Principles: Life and Work by Ray Dalio distills the author's life lessons and management philosophies, offering a practical framework for decision-making and achieving success in both personal and professional spheres.

Buy Principles: The Humanity Factor

The Humanity Factor

The Humanity Factor: A Heart-Driven Approach to Your Finances and Your Future by Frank J. Legan emphasizes aligning financial decisions with personal values and purpose, advocating a compassionate and intentional approach to building a fulfilling future.
